Fire Noodle Eating Champ

FireThe Annual Fire Noodle Eating Contest is about to begin! Contestants will be racing to eat noodles as fast as possible. In real-time, they will be rolling and matching dice of various noodle toppings on their spicy Fire Noodles cards. Once the noodles have their appropriate toppings, they're ready to eat. But don't let the noodles burn on the way down! The first to eat all their bowls of noodles and grab the trophy wins! Fire Noodle Eating Champs: The Dice Game is a game by ToToTam inspired by the video game created by Wombok Games.
